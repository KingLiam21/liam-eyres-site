When I joined Rightmove, the no-show rate for booked meetings was a problem everyone acknowledged but nobody fixed. So I fixed it.

## The Problem

Sales reps were booking meetings, sending a confirmation email, and hoping for the best. Hope is not a strategy.

## What I Built

I pioneered AI integration directly into my mailbox workflow. Here's what that actually looked like:

- **Automated booking confirmations** with smart follow-up sequences
- **AI-generated nudges** sent at optimal times before meetings
- **Dynamic rescheduling prompts** that made it easy for prospects to move a meeting instead of ghosting it

## The Result

Meeting no-shows dropped by 20%. That's not a vanity metric — that's real pipeline that would have evaporated turning into real conversations.

The approach was later adopted across the national team as part of the virtual enablement tools I designed.

## The Takeaway

AI in sales isn't about replacing the human element. It's about removing the friction that stops the human element from happening in the first place.
